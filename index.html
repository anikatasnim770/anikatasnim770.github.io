01 — About Me

Bridging Research & Industry

I'm Anika Tasnim, a Senior Verification Engineer at Ulkasemi Pvt. Limited — Bangladesh's pioneering semiconductor company. Over four years I progressed through three engineering levels, specialising in pre-silicon verification of LPDDR memory interfaces with UVM, SystemVerilog, and Cadence toolchains.

I'm preparing to begin an MSc in Electronic & Communications Engineering (TU203) at Technological University Dublin in September 2026 — with a long-term goal of building a world-class VLSI research lab that connects Bangladesh's semiconductor talent to the Irish and European industry.

Beyond chips: Duke of Edinburgh Gold Award recipient, IEEE author, Ulkasemi DV Vlog creator, and strong believer that diverse voices build better silicon.
